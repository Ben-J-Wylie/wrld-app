Buffer Clip Editor

A clip is a non-destructive cut out of the rolling buffer. Two-zone editor: a full-bleed swipe-to-scrub buffer field over a collapsed-gap, zoomable BufferTimeline with drag brackets. Frames below show the key states. Supersedes the single-waveform Timeline trimmer.
